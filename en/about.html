The Team

ATS is a spin off company from the Traffic Division and the Computer Science Department at Aalborg University. This means we have access to the latest research in both areas. The combination of traffic and computer science makes our team extraordinarily strong as we are able to apply the newest techniques from computer science on the most important issues in traffic.

  • Co-Founder CEO, Andreas Berre Eriksen

    Ph.D. student in computer science with specialisation in machine learning. Andreas was the primary designer of the design of the controllers. Andreas is working on developing the products and is responsible for the day to day operations. Andreas is also co-founder and CEO of the AI company Ambolt IVS which has delivered software to big Danish companies.

    andreas@at-systems.dk

  • Co-Founder CTO, Jakob Haahr Taankvist

    Ph.D. student in computer science with specialisation in modeling and optimizing systems. Jakob is one of the main developers of our optimisation algorithm. Jakobs is working on further developing our products.

    jht@at-systems.dk

  • Co-Founder Senior Advisor, Harry Lahrmann

    Associate Professor in traffic. Harry has a both wide and specialized knowledge about the traffic sector amongst other things in signalized intersections. Harry makes sure our products are viable in the marked and works on general development of these using his intimate knowledge of the marked.

    hsl@at-systems.dk

  • Co-Founder Senior Advisor, Kim Guldstrand Larsen

    Professor of Computer Science. Kim is a world leading researcher in computer science and has been the leader of several huge EU-projects. Kim has amongst many other things received the prestigious ERC Advanced Grant. Kim ensures out products has the highs quality, and that we use the best methods from the field.

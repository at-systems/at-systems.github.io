Announcing AT@Flow

Jan 20 2017

We are happy to announce AT@Flow, based on AI and radar technology AT@Flow is the most advanced traffic control system ever.

The techniques in AT-flow was originally developed and analysed as a research product at Aalborg University, in the DiCyPS project.
We are current working on installing AT@Flow in several crossings e.g. in Aarhus and Aalborg in Denmark, and several in India.
AT@Flow is able to look into the future and using AI it will then optimise the signal setting to get the optimal traffic flow.
One of the biggest advantages of AT@Flow is how easy it is to apply different constraints. Constraints such as minimum and maximum green times, bus prioritisation, and prioritisation of heavy vehicles are easily added to the control. AT@Flow is fully and easily customisable to fit the needs of any road authority.

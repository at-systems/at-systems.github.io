AT-Flow

Our advanced traffic light controller based on the newest research in AI from Aalborg university has shown to reduce queue lengths with up to 60% and the average travel time with up to 50%.
Our controller works in three steps:

  1. Every second it observes the current situation using radars it,
  2. then it looks 20 seconds into the future,
  3. it then uses AI to calculate the optimal signal setting.
